# Vision — HSK Tutor

> Why this product exists.

---

## Problem

Mandarin Chinese is one of the hardest languages for English speakers to learn, and HSK certification (the official Chinese proficiency test) requires mastering thousands of vocabulary words and grammar patterns. Existing tools are either:

- **Too passive** — flashcard apps with no structured learning path
- **Too disconnected** — study materials not tied to the actual HSK exam format
- **Not motivating** — no feedback loops that make daily practice feel rewarding

Most learners plateau or quit because practice feels like rote repetition with no sense of progress.

---

## Solution

HSK Tutor is a focused vocabulary and grammar learning platform that:

1. **Follows the official HSK structure** — content organized by HSK levels 1–6, mirroring the actual exam
2. **Uses spaced repetition (SM-2)** — words resurface at the right time based on individual performance, not fixed schedules
3. **Makes progress visible** — XP, streaks, ranks, achievements, and a leaderboard turn daily practice into a habit
4. **Adapts to the learner** — exercise delivery prioritizes words due for review, then introduces new words at a sustainable pace

---

## Target User

Mandarin learners who:
- Are preparing for HSK certification (levels 1–6)
- Want a structured, daily practice habit
- Prefer a focused tool over an all-in-one language app

---

## Success Looks Like

- A learner can open the app daily, practice for 5–15 minutes, and know they're making measurable progress toward their HSK level
- The SRS system means they never waste time reviewing words they already know solidly
- The gamification keeps them coming back without feeling gimmicky
